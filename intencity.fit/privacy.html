PRIVACY POLICY

UPDATED DATE
May 4, 2016

User Information

Intencity is a fitness application that collects various fitness information from users. This may include:

  • Exercises
  • Sets of an exercise
  • Weight of an exercise
  • Reps or duration of an exercise
  • Intensity of an exercise
  • etc.

How Intencity uses the information

Intencity collects fitness information only to help Intencity's users to achieve their fitness goals. The more information we gather, the better Intencity gets. The better Intencity gets, the easier it is for users to achieve their fitness goals.

Security

Intencity works hard to secure user information. Any data stored with Intencity is stored securely, and any vital information like passwords are hashed to prevent unauthorized parties from obtaining them.

Privacy

Intencity does not and will not sell or give away your information without your consent.

Transparency

People have different privacy concerns. Intencity's goal is clear and will always be transparent with everything it does with user information and data.

Changes

The privacy policy may change in the future, but any changes will be displayed on this page.
